How to play:

  1. First round: You try to guess the computer's 4-digit number
  2. Second round: The computer tries to guess your number
  3. Take turns until one player guesses correctly
  • Dead (red): Correct digit in the correct position
  • Injured (blue): Correct digit but in the wrong position

All digits in the secret numbers must be unique.
